INFRASTRUCTURE AS A SERVICE.

IaaS is a cloud computing model in which a third-party provider delivers infrastructure resources over the internet. This includes physical servers, storage, and networking capabilities. With IaaS, businesses can access the infrastructure they need on a pay-as-you-go basis, without having to purchase and maintain their own hardware. This can save businesses significant upfront costs and ongoing maintenance expenses.

Devtegrate offers IaaS solutions that are scalable, flexible, and secure. Their IaaS offerings include virtual private servers (VPS), which allow businesses to have their own dedicated server resources, as well as shared hosting and cloud storage solutions. Devtegrate's IaaS solutions are designed to meet the needs of businesses of all sizes, from small startups to large enterprises.

PLATFORM AS A SERVICE (PAAS).

DevOps. Cloud Service. Automation.

PaaS is a cloud computing model in which a third-party provider delivers a platform for developers to build, test, and deploy applications. The platform includes all the necessary tools, frameworks, and infrastructure resources needed to develop and deploy applications. With PaaS, businesses can access the tools and resources they need to develop and deploy applications without having to purchase and maintain their own hardware and software.

Devtegrate offers PaaS solutions that are designed to meet the needs of developers and businesses. Their PaaS offerings include a range of tools and resources, such as integrated development environments (IDEs), version control systems, and databases. Devtegrate's PaaS solutions are designed to be flexible and scalable, allowing businesses to easily adapt to changing needs and demands.
